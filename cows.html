Cows

It's time to learn about the sacred cow!

For the rural people of South Sudan, cattle are at the center of their culture. They use them as currency, treat them as objects of beauty, and fight tribal battles over them.

Cows are social animals, and they naturally form large herds. Like people, they will make friends and froms bonds with some herd members, while avoiding others.

Cows have a great sense of smell. They can smell something up to 6 miles away. They enjoy sweet things and can smell their food (that's soaked in molasses and burried) when it is dug up.
