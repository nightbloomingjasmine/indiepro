Private Island Resort

Accomodation

The Resort offers the following rooms and cottages:

  • Regular Room with Double Bed or Twin Beds.
  • Suite with King Bed and Balcony.
  • Studio Cottage.
  • Two Bedroom Cottage.
  • Three Bedroom Cottage.
  • Four Bedroom Cottage.

All rooms are ensuite with a minibar, safe, and basic amenities. Childrens beds available on request.



Activities

The Resort offers the following activities

  • Beach Volleyball
  • Jet Skis
  • Surfing
  • Horse Riding
  • Zip Lining
  • Rock Climbing

More activities available at the Activity Hut on the Resort grounds

















Travel Arangements

The hotel offers the following services

  • Airport to Resort Transfer
  • Airport to Port Transfer
  • Port to Resort Transfer

The resort offers transfers for free; private transfers are available upon request.
